Press-On Nail Sizing Guide 💅🏻

How to Measure

Use a measuring tape (preferably in mm) and place it horizontally across the widest part of your natural nail bed. Measure each nail individually and note down the width.

Size Sets

XS: 3, 6, 5, 7, 9
S: 2, 5, 4, 6, 9
M: 1, 5, 4, 6, 8
L: 0, 4, 3, 5, 7
XL: 0, 3, 2, 4, 6
